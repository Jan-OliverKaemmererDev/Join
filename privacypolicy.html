Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use Join, a project management tool developed as part of a student group project at the Developer Akademie GmbH.

1. Data Collection

We collect personal data that you voluntarily provide when you register for an account, create tasks, or add contacts. This may include:

  • Name and surname
  • Email address
  • Login credentials (encrypted)
  • Profile information (e.g., initials)

2. Purpose of Data Processing

The data is stored and used exclusively for the functionality of the Join application, such as:

  • User authentication and account management
  • Task assignment and collaboration
  • Managing contact lists within the tool

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a secure cloud database (e.g., Firebase) provided by Google. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, please be aware that Join is a student project and not intended for storing sensitive or production-level data.

4. User Rights

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access the data we store about you
  • Request correction or completion of your data
  • Request the deletion of your account and associated data
  • Object to the processing of your data

5. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase (a Google service) for data storage and authentication. Google may process and store data on servers located outside the European Union.

6. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact the project team at jowieja22@gmail.com.
